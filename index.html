The problem

Your team is spending its talent on work that software should be doing — and paying for tools that don't fit how they actually work.

Off-the-shelf platforms are the cheapest line item in a people-operations budget, and the most expensive in practice. They make a company bend its processes to match the software, not the other way around.

What's left over — the multi-state edge cases, the report nobody can build, the workflow held together by a spreadsheet and a calendar reminder — falls back onto the people who can least afford the time.

We build the system that closes that gap.
